KungPao Chicken

Kung Pao Chicken is a classic Chinese dish known for its combination of tender chicken, crunchy peanuts, and a flavorful, slightly sweet and spicy sauce. It often includes vegetables like bell peppers and green onions, creating a delightful balance of textures and tastes.

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless, skinless chicken, diced
  • 1/2 cup unsalted peanuts
  • 2 bell peppers, diced
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce

Cooking Step by Step

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Dice chicken, chop vegetables, and gather peanuts, soy sauce, and hoisin sauce.
  2. Saut√© chicken until cooked, add vegetables, stir in soy sauce and hoisin sauce, and continue cooking until vegetables are tender.
  3. Final Touch: Toss in peanuts, stir briefly, and serve the Kung Pao Chicken over steamed rice.
