Crispy chicken hamburger

Crispy chiken is my wife's favorite! Imagine a toasted sesame seed bun cradling a succulent beef patty, its edges caramelized and juices brimming. Melted cheese drapes over the patty, its gooey embrace welcoming crisp lettuce, juicy tomato, and a tangy whisper of red onion.
This good-good ain't shy. Each bite whispers secrets of perfectly seasoned beef, its char playing counterpoint to the creamy mayo and tangy ketchup peeking from beneath the bun. The crunch of lettuce and tomato adds rhythm, while the onion's sharp kick leaves you wanting more.

Ingredients:

  • Sesame seed buns
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Red onion
  • Cheese

Cooking Step by Step

  1. Sizzle & Stack: Toast your buns, season and form juicy patties, then sear them to perfection. Add cheese for a melty embrace.
  2. Dress to Impress: Pile crisp lettuce, juicy tomato, and tangy onion on the bottom bun. Nestle your cheesy patty on top, then crown it with the other bun.
  3. Unleash the Flavor: Unleash the flavor fiesta with your favorite condiments. Dive in and savor the symphony of textures and tastes!
